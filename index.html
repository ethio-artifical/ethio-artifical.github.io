Dataset Overview

This dataset contains recorded sign language videos collected for the purpose of developing an Ethiopian Sign Language Recognition model. Below is a brief description:

  • Collection Location: Yekatit 23 Primary School and Fasilo Secondary School
  • Content Focus: Family-related topics, such as introductions, jobs, relationships, and social interactions
  • Data Format: Video frames extracted and resized to specific dimensions
  • Annotations: Provided for each video to serve as ground truth for training models
